About Me

I am a Physics student at TU Dortmund University in Dortmund, Germany. Currently I am in my third year of undergraduate studies. My main focus is theoretical physics, I am planning to focus my future career on Theoretical Quantum Physics, Quantum Information and Quantum Statistics in Particular have caught my interest. However, since I have great interest in many fields of physics, I have had the chance to gain experience in experimental physics and other branches of physics as well. Apart from research I am passionate about outreach and showing the beauty within physics and mathmatics to the public.

Research focus topics

Statistical Physics

How do many body systems behave over time? How can we accurately predict their temperature, pressure and other properties without needing to know every particle completely?

Quantum Computing

How can we use the properties of quantum-scale particles for our advantage? Can they be used to exceed the limits of current computers and pave the way to a new era of computer science?

Astrophysics

What secrets are still hidden in our universe? How did that what we know form, how did the early universe behave on a quantum scale and how does our reality of today depend on those starting conditions?

Solid State Physics

What are the defining factors in the behaviour of solids, especially in phase transitions? How can we describe such transitions using statistical methods and what influence does the quantum nature of matter have on them?



What am I looking for?

My main goal at this time is to gain as much research experience in different fields and sub-branches as I can, both in order to prepare for my further academic career (Master & pHD studies) and to contribute to real-world research projects and help the growth of collective knowledge of physical phenomena and their mathematical description. That being said, I am looking to concentrate on theoretical projects since I see my future in that direction. As I am passionate about physics in general the specific branch is not of primary importance to me. It is important to me to be surrounded by a supportive team, ideally working in an international and interdisciplinary environment since such mixings of culture and expertise in my opinion lead to the most creative, and thus successful, projects. I am more than ready to travel for a job - getting to know foreign cultures and workspaces is a great interest of mine.
