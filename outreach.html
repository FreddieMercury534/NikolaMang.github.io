Teaching and Outreach

Both teaching and outreach are of great importance for the scientific community, it is the way we introduce young scientists to the wonders that physics has to offer and inspire them to pursue this field further.

Teaching

I have had the chance to gain some experience in teaching as a tutor in courses on classical mechanics, basic experimental physics and astroparticle physics.

As such, my tasks included the preparation of exercise sheets, the correction and grading of the students' solutions and the discussion of the lectures and students' questions during the tutoring hours.

Outreach

With outreach, we can extend the fascination we have for our research and our subject to the general public and thus further the understanding of the world we live in. Additionally, what is the worth of the greatest discovery if only a handful of people understand what it means?

For this I am glad that I have been offered to participate in the outreach activities of the MAGIC telescopes located on the canary island La Palma, Spain. Especially during the challenging times of the volcanic eruption on La Palma the importance of good science communication was even greater.
